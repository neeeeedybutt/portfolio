About me

I craft designs that resonate!

Growing up in a family of artists ignited a lifelong love of visual expression. With a Bachelor's in Visual Arts, I've developed a keen eye for aesthetics and a deep appreciation for the power of visual storytelling. My design work is rooted in a strong foundation of artistic principles, allowing me to create experiences that are both visually stunning and functionally effective.

I find inspiration everywhere—from the grand masterpieces in museums to the overlooked beauty of everyday life. I spot beauty in the mundane and have an unhealthy obsession with symmetry and abstract compositions. Other than my work, photography has become a recent obsession, allowing me to capture the world through my lens and add another dimension to my creative practice.
